Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

At FRT (Family Road Track), we prioritize your privacy and the security of your family's data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our location tracking app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect location data to provide real-time tracking, journey history, and safe zone alerts. We may also collect account information such as your name, email address, and phone number for account management.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to provide and improve the FRT service. This includes showing real-time location to authorized family members, sending emergency SOS alerts, and analyzing app performance. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data during transmission and storage. Only authorized members of your family group can view your location data based on the privacy controls you configure within the app.

4. Children's Privacy

FRT is designed for family safety. Any tracking of individuals under 13 years old typically requires parental consent as part of the family group setup. Please refer to our parental controls for detailed configuration.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team via the 'Help Center' in the app or at support@familyroadtrack.com.
